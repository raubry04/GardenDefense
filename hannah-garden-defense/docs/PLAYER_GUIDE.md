# Player guide

Welcome to **Hannah's Garden Defense**! Help Hannah and her animal friends stop critters from reaching the garden gate.

## Getting started

1. Open the game on your computer or tablet (see the main [README](../README.md)).
2. Type your name and tap **Play**.
3. On the world map, tap a zone, then tap a battle number to start.

Your progress saves automatically on this device (and syncs when the family server is running).

## How to play a battle

### Before waves start

You get a **prep timer** (about 30 seconds) before the first wave. Use it to place defenders!

1. Tap a **tower card** at the bottom of the screen.
2. Move your finger or mouse over the grass — a ghost animal shows where it will go.
3. Tap a **green-highlighted** tile to place the tower.
4. Red flash means that spot is not allowed (or you need more Sunshine Points).

### During waves

- Enemies follow the dirt path toward the gate.
- Your towers attack automatically.
- Each enemy that gets through costs **hearts** (lives). You start with 20.
- Watch the **wave bar** in the middle of the top HUD.

### Between waves

- You have about **15 seconds** before the next wave.
- The **Next:** row shows which critters are coming — plan ahead!
- Tap **Send Wave** (or **Next Wave**) to start early and earn **+10 bonus** Sunshine Points.

### Sunshine Points ☀

- Yellow number top-right — spend these to place towers during the battle.
- You earn points when enemies are defeated and when waves finish.
- After you win, some points go into your **upgrade bank** for permanent tower improvements.

## Your defenders

| Friend | What they do |
|--------|----------------|
| **Rabbit** | Slows enemies nearby |
| **Chicken** | Throws eggs — great damage (can't hit flying Parrots!) |
| **Dog** | Stuns and slows — good vs fast Gorillas |
| **Owl** | Long range — hits enemies far along the path |
| **Duck** | Slows a wide area |
| **Penguin** | Freezes enemies (needs time to recharge) |
| **Pig Wall** | Blocks the path — only this one can sit on the trail |

New friends unlock as **Hannah levels up** or you reach **new zones** on the map.

## Hannah's special powers

Tap the big round buttons on the **right side**:

| Button | What it does |
|--------|----------------|
| ☀ Sunshine Burst | Hurts all ground enemies |
| 🌧 Garden Rain | Fixes all your towers |
| 🛡 Rainbow Shield | Towers can't be hurt for a short time |
| 💣 Flower Bomb | Big boom (unlocks at Hannah level 6) |

Wait for the circle to fill before using a power again.

## Managing towers

- **Tap a placed tower** to see its stats and range.
- Tap **SELL** to remove it and get half your Sunshine Points back.
- **Upgrades** happen after you win — on the Upgrade screen, not during battle.

## Critters to know

| Critter | Tip |
|---------|-----|
| **Snake** | Easy — good for learning |
| **Frog** | Splits into two Snakes when defeated |
| **Gorilla** | Very fast; Rabbits can't slow it — use Dogs or damage |
| **Parrot** | Flies over the path — use Owls or other targets |
| **Monkey** | Might steal some of your Sunshine Points |
| **Bear** | Walks off the path to smash your towers |
| **Elephant** | Huge and armored — Chickens don't hurt it; use Owls and friends |
| **Cow** | Slow but sturdy (Vegetable Garden) |
| **Horse** | Super fast! (Chicken Coop) |
| **Buffalo** | Breaks through Pig Walls easily (Berry Patch and later) |

The first time you see Cow, Horse, or Buffalo, a gold hint appears — read it!

## Winning and stars

If you protect the gate, you earn **1 to 3 stars**:

- ★★★ — Almost all hearts left (15–20)
- ★★ — Half or more hearts left (8–14)
- ★ — You made it with fewer hearts (1–7)

More stars mean more bonus points for upgrades!

## Pause

Tap the **⏸ button** (top-right) to pause. Choose Resume, Restart, or Back to Map.

## After you win

1. **Upgrade & Continue** — spend banked Sunshine Points to make towers stronger forever.
2. **Back to Map** — pick the next battle or zone.

If critters get through completely, don't worry — the game cheers you on to try again!

## Tips for kids (and grown-ups)

- Put **Rabbits near the start** of the path to slow everything down.
- Save some Sunshine for **later waves** — they get tougher!
- **Send Wave early** when your garden is ready — free bonus points.
- Check the **wave preview** so you know when to place Owls or Pigs.
- Upgrade **Chickens and Owls** between battles for big damage.

Have fun defending the garden! 🌻
